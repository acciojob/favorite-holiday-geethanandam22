Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light", is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. Known for its romantic charm, rich history, stunning architecture, and world-class cuisine, Paris offers an unforgettable experience to every traveler.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic landmark and symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – A beautiful way to view the city’s monuments.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A world-renowned avenue for shopping and dining.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicious French cookies.
  • Escargot – A classic French delicacy of cooked snails.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Cheese – A must for any cheese lover.
