The Himalayas are one of the most majestic mountain ranges in the world, stretching across five countries. Known for their breathtaking landscapes, spiritual significance, and adventure opportunities, the Himalayas attract travelers seeking peace, beauty, and thrill.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Mount Everest – the world’s highest peak.
  2. Leh-Ladakh – famous for its stunning monasteries and adventure sports.
  3. Valley of Flowers – a UNESCO site with rare alpine flora.
  4. Rishikesh – the yoga capital and hub for river rafting.
  5. Darjeeling – known for tea plantations and the Himalayan railway.

Must-Try Foods

  • Momos – delicious steamed dumplings.
  • Thukpa – a warm and comforting Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Butter Tea – a unique salty Himalayan drink.
  • Churpi – a traditional yak cheese snack.
  • Aloo Dum – spiced potato curry popular in the hills.
